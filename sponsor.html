Why Sponsor

Why Sponsor Feather River Grappling?

Feather River Grappling Series was created to bring high-quality jiu-jitsu competition to Oroville and the greater Northern California region. Instead of athletes and families having to travel hours for a well-run tournament, FRG is building a professional local event that supports competitors, teams, families, and the surrounding community.

Sponsorship helps us create a better event experience while giving your business visibility with a highly engaged audience of athletes, parents, coaches, teams, and local supporters.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who attends Feather River Grappling events?

FRG events are designed for youth, teen, and adult competitors, plus coaches, parents, families, teams, and local supporters from Oroville and the greater Northern California region.

Can my business provide products or services instead of cash sponsorship?

In-kind partnerships may be considered when they directly support the event experience. Use the custom partnership option to tell us what you have in mind.

Can sponsors provide flyers or promotional materials?

Yes, most sponsorship packages include the option to provide approved promotional materials for the event table or check-in area.

Are vendor tables available?

Vendor space may be available depending on the event layout and category fit. Approved vendor tables are limited.

Is the Presenting Sponsor package exclusive?

Presenting Sponsor availability may be limited. If your business is interested in top-level visibility, contact us early.
