Questions

FAQ

Do I need an FRG Annual Membership to register for tournaments?

Start by joining the FRG Annual Membership. Membership gives you access to member registration perks, including tournament discount codes and event updates.

Does the $29 membership include tournament entry?

No. Tournament registration is separate and is completed through Smoothcomp for each event.

Where do I register for tournaments?

Tournament registration is handled through Smoothcomp. FRG members receive the current registration link and member discount code through email and text after joining.

What do I get with the FRG Annual Membership?

Members receive registration perks, including $10 off FRG tournament registrations during their active membership year, plus member-first event updates and season champion points eligibility.

Can coaches or parents purchase the membership?

The membership is designed for athletes and families participating in the FRG series. Parents may purchase on behalf of youth athletes.
