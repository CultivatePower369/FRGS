Who We Are

Built for Competitors.
Run by Grapplers.

The Feather River Grappling Series is a community-driven jiu-jitsu tournament organization based in Northern California. We host well-organized, fair, and exciting competitions for all skill levels — from first-time white belts to seasoned veterans. Every event is built with the athlete in mind: smooth brackets, experienced referees, and a culture that respects the art of grappling.
